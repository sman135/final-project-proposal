Spence's Cookie Clicker

Team: Spencer Kolman (solo)

Last Update: 12-10-2020 (sprint 8)

Description

Spence's Cookie Clicker is a simple game that allows you to click a button to add "cookies" to your collection. It also allows you to use upgrades to make each click more powerful by multiplying your default click to add more cookies per click. You can also choose to "prestige" which resets your cookies to 0, but increases your default click by 5 cookies.

Desired Features

  • Displays current amount of cookies and updates the number with every click.
  • Increases to the value of a click
  • Ability to prestige (default click becomes more powerful)

Additional Features

  • Animations
  • Detailed graphics
  • Ability to save progress

Languages and Platforms

Spence's Cookie Clicker uses python, Tkinter, and system libraries.
